\textbf{Question 11.9.1.7} 
Find the indicated terms in the sequence whose nth terms is \(a_n\) = \(4n-3\). Find \(a_{17}\) and \(a_{24}\).

\solution
\begin{table}
    \centering
    \input{tables/table1.tex}
    \caption{Input Parameters}
    \label{tab:table1}
    \end{table}

The function \( x(n) = 4n - 3 \) evaluated at \( n = 17 \) and \( n = 24 \) is given by:
\[
x(17) = 4 \times 17 - 3 = 68 - 3 = 65
\]
\[
x(24) = 4 \times 24 - 3 = 96 - 3 = 93
\]
